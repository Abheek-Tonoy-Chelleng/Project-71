About Delhi Public School Jorhat
One of the finest schools emerging in the service of community, with an ambience unparallel till now, better than the other day schools in Jorhat and neighbourhood with an extensive fifteen acres of premises, stands a very elegantly designed DPS Jorhat. Endowed with Smart Classroom learning technology, modern maths, science, language, computers, robotics, STEM labs,a well stocked library, international standards of outdoor playing courts, a multipurpose performing area , the state-of-art Junior Play School where fun way of learning will take precedence over all school activities, flowing green integrated spaces brought into interaction; a composite knowledge park; all compositely will enhance capabilities of sound health and education. In house and inter school competitions and performances on state-of-art multipurpose stage will give students of DPS Jorhat a window of the outside world and will nurture the budding talents in art, culture, drama, music and dance.Adequate and high quality indoor and outdoor sports facilities will provide adequate health stimulants for complete development of body. The bright , young flowers of the next generation will be nurtured with nutrients of affection, love and care. Yoga and meditation will help young minds to assimilate education while daily morning prayers will invoke the great Lords awakening the impressionable souls in the temple of learning. The campus will cater to the intellectual development through the most modern tools of education available throughout the world.
